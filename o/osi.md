## What is OSI?
Open Systems Interconnection model. Separates networks into seven layers:

|Layer||
|--|--|
| Application ||
| Presentation||
| Session     ||
| Transport   ||
| Network     ||
| Data link   ||
| Physical    ||

A way to remember these is: All People Seem To Need Data Providers.
